### This Power BI dashboard explores injury patterns in professional sports using a rich dataset of player demographics, injury types, locations, and treatment details. The project focuses on uncovering trends, identifying high-risk areas, and supporting better decision-making in injury prevention and recovery planning.
---

## 📌 Project Purpose

To analyze and visualize sports injury data in an interactive, insightful way — helping stakeholders better understand where and why injuries happen, and how to reduce their occurrence and impact.

---

## 🧩 Key Features

- 📊 **Top injury types** highlighted through color-coded bar charts
- 🔄 **Dynamic slicer summary card** that updates based on user selections
- 📍 **Regional injury density maps** for geographic analysis
- ⏱️ **Average recovery time** by injury and treatment type
- 💸 **Cost of treatment** and injury trends over seasons
- 📋 **Detailed injury case explorer** with filterable tables

---

## 🧠 Insights Discovered

- **Fractures** were the most frequent injury type  
- **Indoor courts** had the highest injury counts  
- **Females aged 31–35** showed higher injury incidence  
- **Achilles and rib injuries** had the longest average recovery time  
- **Competition events** led to more injuries and longer recovery than training  
- **2021–22 season** saw the highest injury and treatment cost spikes

## 🧮 DAX Measures Used

### ✅ General Metrics
- `Total Injuries`  
- `Total Players`  
- `Total Cost Incurred`  
- `Avg Days to Recovery`  
- `Injuries per 100 Players`  
- `Recurrent Injury %`  
- `Avg Recovery Days per Treatment`

### 📊 Rankings & Highlights
- `Rank Injuries`  
- `Top 5 Common Injuries` (Color logic: #F17925 for top 5)  
- `Rank Avg Day Injuries`  
- `Fastest Injury Recovery` (Color logic by rank)  
- `Most Effective Treatment Name`

### 👥 Gender-Specific
- `Male Injuries`  
- `Female Injuries`

### 🧠 Smart UX Element
- `Selected Filters`: Displays selected slicer values across:
  - Outcome  
  - Season  
  - Gender  
  - Country  
  - Injury Cause  
  - Severity  
  - Competition Level
